What We Do

At Dynamic Data we provide a highly available monitoring service that alerts via email or text message when an online resource such as a website or application endpoint becomes unreachable. Our mission is to provide accurate alerting with absolutely no false positives so you are completely certain that an alert needs attention instead of trying to figure out if your alerting provider is the problem. We accomplish this through confirming unreachable status through many hosts across multiple countries.
